## Ethics approval statement

This study did not involve human participants, human data, or animals. The
analyses reported in the present version were conducted on a synthetic, machine-
generated corpus and on model-elicited multiple-choice questions; no personal or
identifiable data were collected. Ethics-committee (IRB) approval was therefore not
required for the work reported here.

The manuscript includes a *preregistered protocol* for a future human-subject study
(measuring whether test-takers can exploit the heuristic). That study has **not**
been conducted and will be submitted for institutional ethics review and informed
consent before any human data are collected.
